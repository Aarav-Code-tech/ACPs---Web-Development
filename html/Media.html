Rubiks Cubes


In 1972 Erno Rubik, a 7th grade geometry teacher tried to make a wooden cube for his class but, he ended up making a weird puzzle which he took 1 month to solve. He knew that this would become a famous toy and so, he invented
THE RUBIKS CUBE. The first ever speedcubing competition was held in 1982 in which the world record of 22.95 seconds was created.Since then many new methods to solve the cube faster have been invented and many better and faster cubes have been created.This is a simple tutorial of how to solve it using the layer by layer method-
