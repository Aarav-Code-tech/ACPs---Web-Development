Cricket,

a sport of bat and ball, captivates millions worldwide. Played across various formats – Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 (T20) – it involves two teams of eleven players each, battling it out on a field. The game revolves around batting, bowling, and fielding. 

Batters aim to score runs by hitting the ball, while bowlers try to dismiss them by getting them out. Fielders, positioned strategically, support the bowlers and attempt to catch the ball or run out the batters. With its blend of skill, strategy, and passion, cricket has evolved into a 
global phenomenon
, uniting fans from diverse cultures.
